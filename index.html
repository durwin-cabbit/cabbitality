Questions

What is cabbit.cyou?

A free Lua script for the gamesense.pub cheat for CS:GO, focused on a clean anti-aim suite and minimal visuals.

Is it really free?

Yes. cabbit.cyou is fully free — no keys, no payments, no premium tier.

What version is out?

The current build is the recode — rewritten from scratch for stability and a smaller footprint.

Is it safe to use?

cabbit.cyou runs on top of gamesense — your safety mirrors gamesense's status. We don't touch the game memory directly.

How do I install it?

Download the .lua file, drop it into your gamesense Lua scripts folder, and load it from the menu under "Lua scripts".
